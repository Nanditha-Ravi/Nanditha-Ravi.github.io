Case Study

FSM Neo Flow — No-Code Workflow Platform (0 to 1)

Configurable workflow engine for enterprise operations

0 to 1 Platform No-Code Workflows Enterprise Operations
View Product

Problem

Enterprises needed flexible systems to manage workflows across different use cases, but most solutions required engineering effort for every change. This made onboarding slow, limited adaptability, and created dependency on development teams for even simple workflow updates.

Solution

Built FSM Neo Flow, a no-code workflow platform that allows businesses to configure and manage operations independently by:

  • enabling dynamic creation of tasks, forms, and workflows
  • supporting multiple use cases through a unified platform
  • allowing configuration changes without code deployments

Role (Product Manager)

  • Contributed to building the product from 0 to 1
  • Defined core workflows, use cases, and product structure
  • Owned user stories, backlog, and feature planning
  • Worked closely with design and engineering to ensure usability and scalability

Impact

FSM Neo Flow improved how businesses set up and manage their workflows by reducing dependency on engineering teams and enabling faster configuration changes. This led to quicker onboarding, easier adaptation across use cases, and broader adoption of the platform within operations.

Highlights

  • Built 0 to 1 platform for tasks, forms, and workflows
  • Reduced dependency on engineering through configuration-first design
  • Improved adoption across operational use cases
