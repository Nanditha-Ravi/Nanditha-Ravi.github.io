About

How I approach product.

I work best at the intersection of ambiguity, execution, and customer context. My approach starts with understanding the real problem behind the request, shaping a solution that users can adopt, and then driving it through delivery with clarity across teams.

Across the product lifecycle, I focus on turning insight into decisions: what to build, why it matters, how it creates business value, and what success should look like once it is in customers' hands. I care deeply about user empathy, but I balance that with operational reality, product strategy, and measurable impact.
