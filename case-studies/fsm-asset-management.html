Case Study

FSM Asset Management — Integrated with Neo Flow

Unified solution for workflow-driven asset operations

Asset Workflows Neo Flow Integration Operational Platform

Problem

Many customers were managing assets and operational workflows in separate systems, making it difficult to connect asset data with real-time processes like maintenance, inspections, and service execution. This led to fragmented workflows, limited visibility, and constrained the ability to scale new use cases on top of existing systems.

Solution

Positioned asset management as a core capability within Neo Flow, creating a unified solution that:

  • integrates assets directly into workflows, tasks, and forms
  • enables asset lifecycle management within operational processes
  • allows businesses to configure asset-driven workflows for different use cases

Role (Product Manager)

  • Identified the gap between asset tracking and workflow execution as a business opportunity
  • Defined how asset management fits into the Neo Flow platform
  • Worked with engineering to extend workflows to support asset-based operations
  • Helped shape use cases across multiple customer scenarios

Impact

This integration transformed Neo Flow from a workflow tool into a more comprehensive operational platform. It enabled customers to manage assets within their day-to-day processes, improved visibility and control, and unlocked new use cases across maintenance, inspections, and service operations.

Highlights

  • Integrated asset management into Neo Flow to solve fragmented workflows
  • Enabled businesses to manage assets within operational processes
  • Opened up new use cases across maintenance, tracking, and service workflows
