RTA Dubai Metro App

Digital platform for public transport compliance

Built from proposal to go-live to detect and manage metro and tram violations through a government-scale digital platform.

Government Project 0 to 1 Delivery Violation Detection

Problem

Manual processes for detecting transport violations were inefficient and lacked automation.

Approach

  • Worked from the technical proposal stage onward.
  • Designed violation detection workflows.
  • Coordinated with multiple stakeholders across delivery.

Solution

Built a digital platform to detect and manage metro and tram violations with structured workflows and enforcement support.

Impact

  • Enabled automated violation detection.
  • Improved enforcement efficiency.
  • Successfully delivered a government-scale project.
