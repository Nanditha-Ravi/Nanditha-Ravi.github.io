Case Study

NetSuite Ship Central — Product Enhancements

Shipping workflow enhancements for enterprise fulfillment

Packing Flows Shipping Workflows Fulfillment Systems
View Product

Problem

Ship Central supports complex fulfillment workflows including packing items into cartons, handling multiple orders together, and shipping via integrated carriers. Real-world usage exposed gaps in how these workflows behaved, especially in high-volume scenarios involving multiple orders, packing stages, and shipment processing.

Solution

Enhanced Ship Central workflows to better align with operational needs by:

  • improving packing flows for handling multiple orders and cartons
  • refining shipment processing across app and NetSuite UI
  • ensuring smoother integration with carrier-based shipping and label generation
  • improving visibility into packed items across cartons and shipments

Ship Central enables packing into cartons/pallets and shipping via integrated carriers with label generation and cost visibility.

Role (Product Manager)

  • Identified gaps between product behavior and real-world warehouse workflows
  • Defined enhancements across packing, shipping, and execution flows
  • Worked with engineering and solution teams to implement improvements
  • Validated workflows across different fulfillment scenarios

Impact

These enhancements improved how warehouse teams process orders by making packing and shipping workflows more consistent and aligned with real operational scenarios. This led to smoother execution during high-volume fulfillment, reduced friction in multi-order handling, and improved overall reliability of the shipping process.

Highlights

  • Enhanced packing and shipping across mobile and NetSuite UI
  • Improved multi-order handling and shipment processing
  • Increased efficiency and reliability of order fulfillment
